For Property Managers

Smarter Buildings,
Happier Tenants.

Air quality isn’t just comfort — it’s property
value. We help you track, improve, and
communicate building health.

Tenants expect healthier, modern spaces.
Poor IAQ leads to complaints and higher turnover.
Energy waste drives up operating costs.

What we Offer:
Portfolio-level air monitoring for multiple properties.
Dashboards that give you and tenants peace of mind.
Energy-efficient recommendations for HVAC optimization.

How It Works:
Sensors installed in lobbies, offices, and common areas
→ Central dashboard for all buildings → Monthly reports
for stakeholders → Lower costs + better tenant
satisfaction.

For Offices and Workspaces

Boost Productivity with
Healthier Air.

Poor air quality increases sick days and
drains focus. We help you turn data into a
healthier, more productive office.

Stale indoor air leads to fatigue and lower concentration.
High CO₂ levels and poor ventilation cause productivity loss.
Employers face rising costs from absenteeism.

What we Offer:
Real-time IAQ tracking for offices.
Automatic reports for compliance and wellness programs.
Action plans to optimize HVAC usage and ventilation schedules.

How It Works:
Install sensors → Monitor daily air trends → Get
insights on problem areas → Implement recommended fixes.

For Schools & Universities

Keep Classrooms Safe
and Healthy.

Students and teachers need clean air to learn
and thrive. We make it simple to track and
improve school air quality.

Poor air circulation spreads illness faster.
CO₂ buildup makes classrooms drowsy and lowers test performance.
Parents and administrators demand safer environments.

What we Offer:
Easy-to-use monitoring across classrooms and campuses.
Automated alerts when thresholds are exceeded.
Reports to share with parents, boards, or health inspectors.

How It Works:
Place sensors in classrooms → Get real-time safety
alerts → Review weekly/monthly reports → Apply
recommended fixes.

For Healthcare & Clinics

Protect Patients and Staff with Clean Air.

Airborne risks are higher in healthcare
spaces. We give you the tools to stay
compliant and protect lives.

Pathogens thrive in poorly ventilated areas.
Compliance failures risk fines and reputational harm.
Staff and patients need safe air, 24/7.

What we Offer:
High-accuracy sensors designed for sensitive environments.
Detailed reporting for compliance with health regulations.
Guidance to reduce infection risk and maintain clean air standards.

How It Works:
Sensors installed in key areas → Continuous monitoring →
Automatic compliance-ready reports → Action plans to
reduce risk.

Clarity in every breath.
Data that drives healthier spaces.
