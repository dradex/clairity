Schools & Districts

Create healthier classrooms, improve learning conditions, and provide transparency to staff and parents.

Classrooms are among the most dynamic environments in any building. Occupancy changes every 45–90 minutes, CO₂ spikes rapidly when doors close, and HVAC systems often fail to keep up.

    The Reality in Schools

  • Many classrooms exceed recommended CO₂ limits by mid-morning
  • Teachers report “stuffy” conditions, but facilities have no data to locate the cause
  • Some rooms receive too little outdoor air due to duct balancing issues or aging equipment
  • Portable buildings often have poor ventilation by design
  • Parents and boards increasingly expect transparency and documentation

    How Clairity Addresses These Issues

  • Deploy sensors across classrooms, hallways, and common areas to map ventilation patterns
  • Track CO₂, VOCs, temperature, and humidity continuously during class periods
  • Rank classrooms from healthiest to worst-performing to prioritize interventions
  • Provide clear metrics comparing rooms to ASHRAE 62.1 recommendations
  • Detect rooms that fail to recover between class periods
  • Give leadership real data to present to teachers, parents, and boards
  • Verify improvements after HVAC tune-ups or maintenance visits

    Outcomes

  • Fewer “stuffy room” complaints
  • Healthier learning environments
  • Evidence for maintenance requests, bond proposals, or upgrades
  • Increased trust from staff and parents through transparent, data-backed reporting

Offices & Workspaces

Enhance employee comfort, productivity, and workplace safety with room-by-room IAQ insights.

Modern offices rely on sealed buildings with recirculated air, making ventilation performance critical for comfort and cognitive performance.

    The Reality in Offices

  • CO₂ can reach levels that reduce alertness during long meetings
  • VOC sources (printers, cleaners, carpets, adhesives) spike at unpredictable times
  • Temperature and humidity vary widely across open offices
  • Employees file comfort or odor complaints without actionable data
  • HR and safety teams must verify that workplace conditions meet standards

    How Clairity Improves Indoor Work Environments

  • Highlight conference rooms with CO₂ buildup during meetings
  • Detect VOC increases after cleaning cycles or chemical exposure
  • Identify stagnant zones where airflow fails to circulate
  • Provide office managers with real-time and historical IAQ graphs
  • ocument environmental conditions for HR, safety committees, or building ownership
  • Validate whether HVAC adjustments or duct changes actually improved conditions

    Outcomes

  • Higher productivity and reduced afternoon fatigue
  • More consistent comfort across the building
  • Stronger employee trust through transparent IAQ reporting
  • Clear documentation for occupational health and ESG initiatives

Property Managers & Landlords

Reduce tenant disputes, document building quality, and verify HVAC contractor work with objective IAQ data.

Tenants often report smells, drafts, and “bad air,” but without data, property managers cannot determine if the issue is real, seasonal, or occupant-related.

    The Reality in Managed Properties

  • Complaints are often vague: “It smells weird,” “The air feels heavy”
  • Issues vary between units depending on occupancy, furniture layout, and duct balancing
  • Traditional HVAC checks do not capture real-time indoor conditions
  • No objective proof exists to resolve disputes or assess contractor performance
  • Multiple buildings make consistent tracking nearly impossible

    How Clairity Supports Property Management

  • Deploy sensors to specific units or suites experiencing complaints
  • Provide unit-to-unit comparisons to identify localized issues
  • Track VOCs for cleaning chemical use, off-gassing, or odors
  • Generate timestamped graphs to show how IAQ changes throughout the day
  • Provide documented evidence to tenants during disputes
  • Verify contractor work with before-and-after monitoring
  • Manage multiple buildings through a centralized portfolio dashboard

    Outcomes

  • Faster resolution of tenant complaints
  • Reduced liability through documented IAQ records
  • Lower maintenance guesswork
  • Improved tenant satisfaction and retention

Gyms & Fitness Facilities

Maintain proper air turnover, reduce odors, and ensure safer indoor exercise conditions.

Gyms have some of the most intense indoor air loads of any commercial environment due to heavy breathing, sweat, high traffic, and cleaning cycles.

    The Reality in Fitness Spaces

  • CO₂ rises quickly during classes or peak workout times
  • VOCs increase from cleaners, rubber mats, and sweat
  • Weight rooms and corners often have stagnant air
  • Members judge facility quality by smell and airflow
  • Complaints are common but hard to measure objectively

    How Clairity Improves Gym Air Quality

  • Track rapid CO₂ increases during high-intensity activity
  • Identify poorly ventilated areas or airflow dead zones
  • Detect VOC and odor spikes from cleaning or off-gassing materials
  • Provide real-time dashboards for operators and trainers
  • Validate HVAC improvements after maintenance or duct adjustments
  • Show members transparent IAQ data to build confidence

    Outcomes

  • Fresher, safer-feeling workout spaces
  • Fewer odor and ventilation complaints
  • Better air turnover during classes
  • Higher member satisfaction and retention
