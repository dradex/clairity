Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/24/2025
Last Updated: 11/24/2025

1. Introduction

Clairity (“we,” “our,” or “the Company”) provides indoor air-quality (IAQ) analysis and monitoring services using portable sensors, offline data collection, and analytical reporting. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and protect information when clients access our services or interact with Clairity equipment.

2. Information We Collect

Environmental Sensor Data: CO₂, VOC index, temperature, humidity, uptime, and diagnostics. Sensors do not collect personal data.
Client/Admin Information: Contact details, organization name, room identifiers, deployment notes, billing information.
Website Analytics: Basic traffic metrics and cookies used solely for performance and security.

3. How Information Is Used

We use collected data to generate IAQ reports, improve performance, support deployments, communicate with clients, and ensure system reliability. We do not sell or trade data.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Environmental data is stored securely and retained for 12 months for audits or for the duration of monitoring contracts. Clients may request deletion of administrative data.

5. Data Protection

We implement encrypted storage, isolated networks, access restrictions, and secure hardware practices. While we take extensive measures, no system guarantees absolute security.

6. Sharing of Data

Data may be shared only with authorized client personnel, vetted vendors under confidentiality agreements, or when required by law. We never share data with advertisers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Clairity does not collect personal information from children. IAQ metrics are environmental and non-identifying.

8. Client Rights

Clients may request access, correction, or deletion of stored administrative data. All requests may be submitted to: milan.c@clairity.info.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect regulatory or operational changes. Updates take effect upon publication.
