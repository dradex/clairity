A Complete Indoor Air Quality Lifecycle—From Detection to Resolution

Clairity provides real-time indoor air quality intelligence for schools and small commercial buildings.

From portable sensor deployment to automated reporting and optional HVAC remediation, Clairity delivers the full journey of identifying, diagnosing, and solving ventilation and air-quality problems.

Simple, Fast Sensor Installation

Portable sensors that work anywhere. No IT involvement. No drilling.

Clairity sensors are designed for schools and offices—simple, safe, and completely self-contained.
Place them in classrooms, offices, conference rooms, hallways, or any space you want to evaluate.

  • No permanent installation or wiring
  • Works even if building Wi-Fi is unavailable
  • Local data logging for continuous capture
  • Move sensors from room to room during audits

Autonomous Data Collection

Continuous CO₂, VOC, temperature, and humidity tracking.

Once placed, sensors begin collecting environmental data around the clock. Data uploads automatically whenever the travel router is powered—no manual downloads required.

  • eCO₂
  • VOC index (chemical pollutants, odors, cleaning agents)
  • Occupancy-driven patterns
  • Hour-to-hour ventilation performance

Data Processing & Intelligent Metrics

Raw data is converted into clear, meaningful insights.

Clairity’s cloud processing engine analyzes thousands of datapoints from each room and generates standardized performance metrics that are easy to interpret.

    Metrics Generated:

  • Average CO₂ levels vs ASHRAE 62.1 recommendations
  • VOC patterns and anomaly detection
  • Daily ventilation cycle analysis
  • Air-change behavior during peak occupancy
  • Comfort index (temperature & humidity stability)
  • Room-by-room health score

    Clairity automatically identifies:

  • Rooms with persistent CO₂ buildup
  • Ventilation failures or airflow imbalance
  • Spaces with chemical/pollutant spikes
  • Rooms that recover too slowly after occupancy
  • Areas consistently outside recommended comfort ranges

Real-Time Dashboard

Every room. Every metric. Clearly visualized.

Your dashboard gives a complete picture of how your building breathes—hour by hour, day by day.

  • Room rankings (best → worst air quality)
  • Daily trend graphs for CO₂, VOCs, temp, humidity
  • Anomaly detection flags
  • Before/after comparisons for HVAC or maintenance changes
  • Multi-building support for districts or portfolios
  • Weekly summary reports sent automatically

Problem Detection & Automatic Alerts

Clairity pinpoints exactly where and why problems occur.

    When the system detects abnormal patterns, such as:

  • Persistent CO₂ spikes
  • Insufficient ventilation during class/office hours
  • VOC elevations from materials or products
  • Rooms that fail to return to baseline air quality
  • Comfort instability (temperature & humidity swings)

    • …it highlights those rooms and notifies you via dashboard alerts and weekly summaries.


    This eliminates guesswork and prevents issues from going unnoticed for months.

Automatically Generated IAQ Reports

Clear documentation for leadership, staff, parents, or tenants.

    Clairity produces full IAQ reports complete with:

  • CO₂ trend breakdowns
  • VOC pattern summaries
  • Comfort analysis
  • Room-ranking overview
  • Identified issues and root-cause indicators
  • Recommended corrective actions
  • Before/after change verification

    • Reports are written in plain language so non-technical stakeholders can easily understand the findings.

    Perfect for:

  • Principals & district administrators
  • Facility managers
  • Health and safety teams
  • Property managers
  • Tenant communications

Optional HVAC Remediation Support

When needed, we connect you with trusted HVAC professionals.

If a ventilation or air-quality issue requires mechanical intervention, Clairity can coordinate with qualified HVAC contractors to assess and resolve the problem.

    Optional HVAC Support Includes:

  • Sharing IAQ data with contractors
  • Identifying likely mechanical causes
  • Scheduling on-site assessments
  • Implementing repairs or ventilation adjustments
  • Verifying improvements through follow-up monitoring

    • Clairity ensures issues are not only detected—but actually fixed and validated.
