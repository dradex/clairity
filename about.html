ABOUT

OUR STORY

Clairity began with a simple question: “Why is indoor air still invisible?”

Our founders saw how poor indoor air quality was affecting schools, workplaces, and healthcare facilities — driving illness, lowering productivity, and wasting energy.

Traditional monitoring was either too expensive, too complex, or too slow to provide real answers.

So we set out to build something different.

We combined expertise in HVAC engineering, environmental science, and data analytics to create a platform that makes the invisible visible. Clairity turns complex indoor air data into clear, actionable insights that organizations can use immediately.

From our first pilot project in 2025 to serving 12 clients today, our mission has stayed the same:

Help organizations breathe smarter, work healthier, and save energy — all at once.

WHY IT MATTERS

Indoor air is invisible, yet it impacts everything. Poor IAQ can cause fatigue, headaches, asthma triggers, and even long-term health risks.

In schools, it lowers student performance;
in workplaces, it reduces productivity;
in healthcare facilities, it affects recovery and patient outcomes.

Most organizations don’t realize the cost of bad air until it’s too late —
higher sick days, energy waste, and compliance risks.


Clairity exists to change that.

OUR VISION

We believe the spaces where people learn, heal, and work should always support health and performance.

Our vision is to make clean, healthy, and efficient indoor air a standard, not a luxury.

By combining advanced monitoring with actionable insights, we’re building a future where every building breathes smarter — protecting people, boosting productivity, and reducing environmental impact at the same time.
