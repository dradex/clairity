Pricing FAQ

Q: Do I need IT approval?

No. Clairity operates off-building with its own router.

Q: How long does an audit take?

Most rooms require 24–72 hours of monitoring.

Q: Can I rent sensors for ongoing use?

Yes. We offer long-term leasing for districts and offices.

Q: Do you offer discounts for multiple rooms/buildings?

Yes. Volume pricing is available—contact us.

Q: What if I only want to test one room?

Single-room audits are available with full reporting.

Q: Is this safe for classrooms or offices?

Yes. Clairity sensors are low-voltage and require no installation, drilling, or Wi-Fi access. All equipment operates independently from building systems.
